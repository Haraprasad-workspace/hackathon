Frequently Asked Questions

What is e-waste, and why is it important to dispose of it properly?

E-waste includes discarded electronic devices like old phones, computers, and appliances. Proper disposal prevents environmental damage and health risks caused by toxic materials like lead and mercury.

How does this platform help in e-waste management?

Our AI-powered platform connects rural citizens with authorized e-waste recyclers, offers educational resources, and provides pickup scheduling for easy disposal.

Who can use this platform?

Any citizen of the Rajkot District can use this platform to dispose of e-waste. Registered recyclers with GPCB can also use it to collect and process waste.

Is there a cost for using the e-waste pickup service?

No, our platform provides free e-waste pickup services in designated rural areas. However, in some cases, recyclers may offer incentives for certain types of e-waste.

What happens to my e-waste after collection?

The collected e-waste is sent to registered recyclers who extract valuable materials and dispose of hazardous components in an environmentally friendly way.

How can I schedule an e-waste pickup?

Simply log in to the platform, select "Schedule Pickup," enter your location, and choose a convenient time. Our AI will assign the best route for recyclers.
